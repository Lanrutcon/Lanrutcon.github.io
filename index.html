About Me

My name is Filipe and I like coding. Currently, I'm in the last year of IT (Java is the main language on my college) and when I'm not studying and I'm usually playing games, one of them is World of Warcraft. I started looking at addOns' code a year ago and soon I started to make small changes. After some time, I developed my first addOn, 1000Eyes. Today, I've done a handful of them, as well as backports to 4.3 patch. My main objective is to help others, always sharing my knowledge, trying to get more people aboard the "code" train.

At the moment, I'm playing World of Warcraft on Atlantiss. It's a Blizzlike server with 4.3.4 patch (Cataclysm). Considered by many, the best Cataclysm private server out there.
